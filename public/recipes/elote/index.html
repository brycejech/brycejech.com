Elote

July 22, 2022

Grilled corn slathered in a sauce of sour cream, mayonnaise, and chili powder, and sprinkled with cotija cheese and cilantro. A popular snack served by street vendors in Mexico, so it's often called Mexican street corn. When served in a cup it is referred to as esquitas or elote en vaso.

Ingredients

6 ears Corn
1 tablespoons Butter
1/4 cups Mayonnaise
1/4 cups Sour Cream
1/2 cups Cotija Cheese
3 tablespoons Cilantro
1 tablespoons Lime Juice
1 tablespoons Chile Powder
