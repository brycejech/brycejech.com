Easy Beef "Stroganoff"

July 22, 2022

Traditionally made with braised or seared cuts of meat, this alternative to a more typical beef stroganoff recipe makes for a quick, easy, and delicious weeknight meal.

Ingredients

24 ounces Reames Egg Noodles
