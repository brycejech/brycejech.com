Genius Stew

September 11, 2022

Formerly named Guiness stew, this is the greatest and last vegetable beef stew you'll ever need

Ingredients

2 lbs Ground beef
16 ozs Bacon
1.5 lbs Red potato
250 grams Celery
200 grams Carrots
300 grams Onion
6 ozs Tomato paste
50 ml Lemon juice
50 ml Apple Cider Vinegar
1000 ml Water
2 Tbsp Beef bouillon paste
2 Tbsp Garlic bouillon paste
1.5 bottles Guiness extra stout
1.25 cups Flour (to thicken)
1 can LeSueur peas
2 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
