Green Bean Casserole

October 13, 2024

Green bean casserole with alfredo instead of cream of mushroom

Ingredients

4 cans Green Beans
18 ounces Bacon
1 cup Alfredo Sauce
1/2 cup Milk
1 stick Butter
1 cup French onions
1 cup Medium cheddar cheese

Directions

Combine green beans, bacon, alfredo, milk, and butter into a baking dish. Cover and cook for 30 min at 350 deg. Add french onions and cheddar cheese, cook uncovered at 450 deg another 15 minutes.
