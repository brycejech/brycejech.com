Orlando Trip

The family took a trip to Orlando, FL for Wylie to compete in the International Geography Bee.

We flew out on June 15th 2022 and returned on the 21st. This was the kids’ first plane ride.

Activities:

  • Pirate Putting
  • Escape room
  • Universal Studios
  • Icon Park
    • Swing ride
    • Zombie killing
    • Tin roof
